Curriculum in Action

Lakewood Schools is proud to be among the institutions in Kenya committed to delivering the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) with excellence and innovation. Guided by our vision of shaping well-rounded learners, we have fully integrated CBC principles into our teaching methodologies.

Through continuous teacher training and professional development, we equip our educators with the skills and strategies needed to deliver lessons that are engaging, learner-centered, and results-driven. This ensures that every student not only acquires knowledge but also builds essential competencies such as critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication.

At Lakewood, we embrace blended learning by integrating technology into the classroom. Digital tools create opportunities for learners to practice independently, collaborate with peers, and receive personalized support where needed. With enrichment activities, co-curricular engagement, and differentiated teaching, we ensure that no learner is left behind. Every child is supported to grow at their pace, unlock their potential, and step into the future with confidence.

Gudiance

At Lakewood Schools, we believe that true education goes beyond academics. Through our strong guidance and pastoral care program, we nurture the emotional, social, and spiritual well-being of every learner. Our dedicated team of teachers and mentors ensures that each child feels supported, valued, and encouraged to grow into a confident, responsible, and compassionate individual. With counseling, mentorship, and life-skills training, we provide a safe environment where learners can thrive both inside and outside the classroom

Character Development

CBC in Kenya emphasizes not just academics but also nurturing values and life skills, and at Lakewood Schools, we take this mission seriously. Our learners are guided to grow into disciplined, empathetic, and confident individuals, ready to take on life's challenges with resilience and integrity.

Beyond the Classroom: Community Engagement

we make every effort to identify and nurture each learner’s unique talents and abilities. This is achieved through a wide variety of clubs, societies, and co-curricular activities. Learners are given opportunities to showcase their skills during talent shows, exhibitions, and entrepreneurship days. In addition, they actively participate in competitions such as drama, music festivals, sports, and academic congresses.

We also organize Career Days where professionals are invited to mentor and create awareness about diverse career opportunities, helping learners make informed choices as they prepare for Senior School pathways.

To complement academic learning, we run vibrant enrichment programs that allow learners to apply classroom knowledge to real-life situations. These programs build creativity, innovation, leadership, and problem-solving skills while fostering teamwork and discipline.
