About Lakewood Schools/

History

Lakewood Schools was founded with a vision to provide quality, holistic education that blends academic excellence with character development. From its humble beginnings, the school has grown into a network of campuses across Kenya, offering world-class facilities and nurturing environments where students thrive under the CBC curriculum. Our journey reflects a commitment to innovation, inclusivity, and preparing learners to become responsible leaders of tomorrow.

Achivements

At Lakewood Schools, we celebrate milestones that reflect our pursuit of excellence. Over the years, our students have excelled in academics, co-curricular activities, and national assessments under the CBC curriculum. Beyond academics, our learners have represented the school in sports, arts, and innovation competitions, earning recognition both locally and internationally.

Timeless values

At Lakewood Schools, our foundation rests on timeless values that shape not just students but future citizens of the world.

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program focuses on early childhood development, fostering creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning in a nurturing environment.

Primary

Our primary education program provides a strong foundation in core subjects, critical thinking, and social skills, preparing students for future academic success.

Secondary

Our secondary education program offers a comprehensive curriculum, extracurricular activities, and college preparation to equip students for higher education and career opportunities.

Programmes

At Lakewood Schools, we offer a variety of academic and co-curricular programmes designed to nurture the whole child. Each programme is tailored to support the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) while encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and personal growth.

Facilities

At Lakewood Schools, our campuses are equipped with world-class facilities that support both academic excellence and holistic growth. Every space is designed to inspire curiosity, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Trusted By

Lakewood Schools has earned the trust of parents and students nationwide by consistently delivering academic excellence, strong values, and holistic growth. With campuses spread across Kenya, we are proud to be a school community that families rely on for quality education.
